Our Approach

📋

Diagnostic Assessment

We begin with a diligently curated diagnostic to understand exactly what you struggle with. This personalized analysis identifies your specific weaknesses and learning patterns, creating a foundation for targeted improvement.

📚

Concept Mastery

We teach the concepts you don't understand with clear explanations and real-world examples. No topic is too complex—we break it down step by step until you have a deep, foundational understanding.

🎯

Overtraining

We push you beyond the SAT by solving unrealistically challenging problems that test the exact same concepts. Through exposure to confusing and complex question structures that mirror the SAT's trickiest phrasing, you'll learn to spot every trap and trick. This overtraining makes even the hardest SAT problems look easy once you're ready for test day.
